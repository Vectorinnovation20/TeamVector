ABOUT US

Team Vector is part of the Motorsports club of Presidency University, Bengaluru,Karnataka. This team comprises of passionate and enthusiastic engineering students from various branches like Electronics, mechanical and computer science. The main focus of our team is on the motorsports event which gives our team the opportunity to demonstrate and prove both our creativity and engineering skills. This helps us to fulfill our desire to design fabricate and race with our own go-kart. The team not only focuses on building e-kart but also look after the management and business development aspects of the team.Till date our team has participated in Edgeline E-kart championship 2020-21, secured runner-up in business plan presentation. We are constantly learning and evolving go kart racing team with innovations, who aims to create a team with absolute team spirit and leadership quality. The team also looks forward to expand their horizon by participating in other events apart from go-kart too.
